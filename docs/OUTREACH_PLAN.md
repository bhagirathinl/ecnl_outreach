- ‚úÖ Send initial introduction emails

- Mention you'll be at ECNL Phoenix

- Thank them for coming
- Don't be pushy

**2-3 Days After Event (Nov 25-26):**
- Send thank you email
- Share event performance/stats
- Request feedback on fit
- Ask about next steps in their recruiting process

## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Personalize Each Email:** Research each program - mention recent success, coaching style, academic programs
2. **Be Professional:** Keep emails concise, error-free, respectful
3. **Follow Up Appropriately:** Don't spam, but don't be afraid to follow up
4. **Be Patient:** Coaches are busy, responses may take days/weeks
5. **Stay Positive:** This is a process - every contact is valuable experience
